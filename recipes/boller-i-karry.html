Baller i Karry

Description

Baller i Karry is the comfort food you need after a long night of drinking Tuborg Classic and singing Kim Larsen. The flavour of the creamy curry sauce mixed with the rice and pork packs more of a punch then then this dish's boring appearance leads on.

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 400 grams of minced pork (of good quality)
  • 1 onion
  • 1 large egg
  • 4 tablespoons of flour
  • Salt and pepper
  • 500 ml chicken stock
    •

    Boller i Karry

  • 2 tbsp curry - remember to taste as curry mixes are very different
  • 1 tablespoon turmeric (can be omitted)
  • 75 grams of butter
  • 250 ml chicken stock (use the one you cooked the meatballs in)
  • 2.5 dl milk (preferably whole milk)
  • 3 tablespoons of flour
  • Salt
  • Possibly piment d'espelette or cayenne pepper (just half a teaspoon)
  • Your family's favourite rice

    • Steps

    Meatballs

    1. Peel and finely chop the onion
    2. Mix the pork, chopped onion, flour and egg and stir it into a smooth stuffing
    3. Add salt and pepper
    4. Form your minced meat into meatballs - use a tablespoon to help you
    5. Heat some water or chicken stock to the boiling point and pour your meatballs in
    6. Cook for approximately 8 minutes
    7. Set aside until they are in the curry sauce

    Boller i Karry

    1. Melt butter in a pot or saucepan and add curry and turmeric - let it fry for half a minute
    2. Add Add flour and whisk together
    3. Add the chicken stock little by little while continuing to whisk - the consistency should be a little too thick for you to want to eat it
    4. Stir the curry sauce with a little milk and season with salt and pepper. Possibly a little piment d'espelette or cayenne pepper
    5. Pour your meatballs into the sauce and let them stand for a few minutes at medium heat until they are heated through and ready to eat
    6. Serve with rice or bulgur
