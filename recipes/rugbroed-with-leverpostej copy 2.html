Rugbroed with Leverpostej

Description

Leverpostej and rugbroed is God's gift to the culinary arts. The intermingling tastes of dense bread and rich leverpostej will leave you questioning where and when did Northern American society go so wrong. Two pieces in and you'll be scratching at the Danish permanent Visa office begging to fill out an application for permanent residency.

Ingredients

  • Leverpostej
  • Rugbroed

    • Steps

    1. Remove the freshly baked loaf of rugbroed from the oven
    2. Slice it into perfectly scrumptious 1cm slices
    3. Place slices on plate and mentally prepare for what comes next
    4. Remove leverpostej from refrigerator
    5. Gently open the lid from the container, basking in the exquisite smell
    6. Spread the leverpostej over the warm bread an watch it melt before your eyes
    7. When you can't resist any longer, bite into it and let your mind explode
