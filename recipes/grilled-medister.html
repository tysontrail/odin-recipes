Grilled Medister

Description

Move over German sausages, Grilled Medister is the here to stay. This swirly meat popsicle is packed full of spices and flavor. But don't get dizzy trying to see where it starts and ends, pop this bad boy on the grill and prepare for a taste explosion!

Ingredients

  • Medister
  • 4 tablespoons acacia honey
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons thyme leaves

    • Steps

    1. Poke Poke small holes in the medister sausage with a needle so that it does not split
    2. Stir acacia honey, mustard and thyme together
    3. Brush the medister with the mixture
    4. Plug if necessary 2 wooden skewers through the medister - they hold it together and make it easier to turn it.
    5. Grill the medister for 8-10 minutes by indirect heat on the grill
    6. Brush again with the marinade and turn the medister sausage
    7. Grill for a further 8-10 minutes
    8. Let the medister rest before serving
